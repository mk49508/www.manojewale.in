Modal Auxiliaries

1. Use of Shall - I और We के साथ shall को प्रयोग होता है। यदि second या third person का प्रयोग shall के साथ हो तो यह Command या Promise का संकेत करते हैं; जैसे-
You shall go to school today. (command)
He shall have his share positively. (promise)
यदि shall के साथ second और Third Person का प्रयोग होता है, तो यह कभी-कभी Threat और Determination (निश्चय) का भी संकेत देता है; जैसे-
You shall admit your mistake one day. (threat)
He shall pay for that. (threat)
I think you shall not deny. (Determination)
He shall keep his promise. (Determination)
नोट -प्रश्न-सूचक वाक्य में सदैव First Person के साथ shall ही आता है; जैसे-
Shall I also come there?
l Shall we go?
2. Use of will Second or Third person के साथ will का प्रयोग होता है; जैसे-
You will ot mind.
He will see me tomorrow.
यदि will का प्रयोग First Person के साथ किया जाता है तो वह इच्छा या प्रतिज्ञा के लिए आता है। जैसे-
I will meet him in the office. (willingness)
I will settle all the dispute tomorrow. (Promise)
इसका प्रयोग threat or determination के लिए भी होता है; जैसे-
I will shoot him if he does not keep his promise. (threat)
We will have our own share. (determination)
3. Would - यह will की past form है। वैसे तो यह will के नियम अनुसार ही प्रयोग में लाया जाता हैं, फिर भी इसका प्रयोग subordinate clause और Indirect speech में past tense में होता है; जैसे-
He said that he would not follow his order.
I told him that I would teach them.
इसका प्रयोग Request में भी होता है; जैसे-
Would you help me in this case?
Would he lend me your book for today only?
इसका प्रयोग कभी-कभी असंभव बात के लिए भी होता है। जैसे-
Would that I was his friend!
Would that he was a beggar!
4. Should - इसके प्रयोग के नियम shall के समान हैं और shall की past form 'should' हो जाती है। 'should' सदैव duty को बताता है। जैसे-
We shold help the poor.
You should not miss this chance.
यह Future event के लिए भी प्रयुक्त होता है; जैसे-
She said that I should keep my promise.
The engineer said that the road should be repaired.
'lest' (ऐसा न हो कि) के साथ सदैव 'should' आता है; जैसे-
Work hard lest you should fail.
Work slowly lest you should fail.
5. May- This modal is used generally for permission; (आज्ञा, उद्देश्य, सम्भावना, आशिर्वाद या अभिशाप प्रकट होता है।)eg.
You may go there.
May I come in, sir.
He may come tomorrow. (Possibility)
I may go there to collect the money. (Purpose)
May you live long! (wish)
6. Might - It is past tense of may (यह may का past tense है।) eg.
He might have been a good lowyer. (possibility)
You might catch the train up to ten. (Suggestion)
Ravi work hard so that he might pass the examination (Purpose)
I hope that you might succeed. (wish)
7. Can- It show the power and authority; eg.
I can beat him. (Power)
He can suspend the employer. (Authority)
8. Could- Past tense of 'can'; eg.
He could not return the loan of the bank in time.
He could not achieve the target.
9. Ought - It is used in moral obligation and is always followed by 'to'; (यह नैतिक कर्तव्य दर्शाता है यह to के साथ आता है।)eg.
He ought to go there for the service.
You ought to serve the poor.
10. Need (जरूरत)-यह आवश्यकता, निषेध या प्रश्नसूचक वाक्यों में प्रयुक्त किया जाता है। जैसे-
You need not to go there.
Need she come here?
11. Must- It shows duty with out fail (कर्तव्य दर्शाता है) ; eg. You must help your parents. (not should)
You must not to touch my things.
He must work hard to pass the examination.
I must not forget his obligations.
12. Dare(साहस करना) - It means to challenge; eg.
She dares to beat a man.
He dared to jump into a well.
13. Used to - It shows the habit generally, it shows the past actions; (यह भूतकाल मे आदत को दर्शाता है।) eg.
He used to drink the wine.
I used to go to school late.
The English teacher used to come late.
He used to tell a lie always.
